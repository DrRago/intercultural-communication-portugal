Cultural Ranking

Trompenaars

  1. Universalism vs. particularism
  2. Individualism vs. communitarianism
  3. Specific vs. diffuse
  4. Neutral vs emotional
  5. Achievement vs. ascription
  6. Internal vs. external control
  7. Sequential vs. synchronous time
  1. one of the most universalistic
    • laws are fixed
  2. center, more communitarianistic (commonly think of themselves as we)
  3. center (business life and private life are equally important)
  4. center, but more neutral (show less emotions)
  5. center, but more achievement oriented (performance = status)
  6. definitely external controlled
    • guided by god
    • work with their environment
    • Be there for others, positively
  7. no information, but since it’s high context: Synchronous (multitasking)
    • more of a present oriented culture
